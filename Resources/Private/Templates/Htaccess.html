Deny direct access. That means we need to be redirected through a RewriteRule (which means %{ENV:REDIRECT_STATUS} must not be empty). RewriteCond %{ENV:REDIRECT_STATUS} ^$ RewriteRule .* - [F,L] ExpiresActive on ExpiresByType text/html {mode}{lifetime} RewriteEngine On RewriteCond % >@{expires} RewriteRule ^.*$ /index.php
